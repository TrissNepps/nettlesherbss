Belly Binding

Breastfeeding support
The wrap makes it difficult to slouch which is common when breastfeeding.

Core support
Binding helps to bring the abdominal muscles back together if there is any diastasis recti present.

Pelvic floor support
Aids in keeping those pelvic muscles together after naturally loosening during birth.

Emotional support
Belly binding is a form of self care for the new mother ~ honoring her, as well as wrapping her up to provide support & comfort.

Physical support

Postpartum waist girth
The pressure from the wrap assists in ridding any water retention or swelling postpartum, as well as providing shape & support for the body.

Belly binding provides physical, as well as emotional support for the new mom. It is a sacred art that is perfect for honoring the new mother and her body; she will feel supported as well as nurtured after each wrap session.
